INTRODUCTION TO WEB DEVELOPMENT

Welcome

In this course of Introduction to Web development.
We will be breaking down the basics of HTML & CSS and at the end of the learning we will make use of the knowledge of what was learnt to build a mini-blog.
This will aid you on how to apply the basics syntax learnt to building something and in future purpose while going advanced you'd be triggered to build amazing stuff.

PS: For everything learnt it takes lot of practice be really good at it.
We are all prone to mistakes even an experienced developer does to. The ability for you to be able to search and search,tackling your curiosity is what makes you get better.
Learn to make use of Google and never be shy of asking any question, it doesn't make you stupid rather it broadens your knowledge and understanding.
Our goal is to make it as easy as possible for complete beginners to become professional web developers, so if you’ve never written a line of HTML or CSS, but you’re contemplating a career shift.
